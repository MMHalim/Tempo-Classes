Tempo Classes Gallery

HIIT

HIIT classes feature dynamic workouts that combine short bursts of intense exercise with brief recovery periods, incorporating movements like sprinting, bodyweight exercises, and resistance training. They boost cardiovascular fitness and metabolism, making them ideal for burning calories and improving strength in a short time. Suitable for all fitness levels, these classes create a motivating environment that encourages participants to push their limits and reach their goals

Weightlifting

Weightlifting classes focus on building strength and technique through resistance training exercises, including foundational lifts like squats, deadlifts, and bench presses. Suitable for all fitness levels, these classes enhance muscle tone, boost metabolism, and improve overall fitness. Participants benefit from expert guidance in a supportive environment, helping them achieve their strength goals while developing confidence and skills.

Bodyweight Strength

Bodyweight strength training utilizes the individual’s own weight as resistance to build muscle and enhance overall fitness. This method includes exercises like push-ups, squats, lunges, and planks, making it accessible for all fitness levels and requiring minimal equipment. Bodyweight training improves strength, endurance, and flexibility while promoting functional movement and core stability. Ideal for those seeking a convenient and effective workout, it can be done anywhere, making it a versatile option for building a strong, toned body.

Bodyweight Cardio

Bodyweight cardio workouts focus on elevating the heart rate using the body’s own weight, incorporating exercises like jumping jacks, burpees, high knees, and mountain climbers. These high-energy routines improve cardiovascular endurance, burn calories, and enhance overall fitness without the need for equipment. Suitable for all fitness levels, bodyweight cardio can be performed anywhere and easily adapted to individual goals.

Barre

Barre is a low-impact workout that blends elements of ballet, Pilates, and yoga to enhance strength, flexibility, and posture. Classes typically focus on small, controlled movements at a ballet barre, targeting key muscle groups, especially in the legs, core, and glutes. With an emphasis on alignment and technique, barre workouts improve overall body awareness and toning while promoting a graceful, lean physique.

Yoga

Yoga is a holistic practice that combines physical postures, breath control, meditation, and mindfulness to promote overall well-being. It enhances flexibility, strength, and balance while reducing stress and improving mental clarity. With various styles ranging from gentle and restorative to vigorous and challenging, yoga accommodates all fitness levels and preferences. Classes often focus on aligning the body and calming the mind, fostering a deeper connection to oneself.

Pilates

Pilates is a low-impact exercise method focused on core strength, flexibility, and body alignment. Through controlled movements and breathing, it enhances muscle tone, stability, posture, and balance. Classes often use specialized equipment like the reformer and include mat exercises, making it ideal for rehabilitation and injury prevention. With its emphasis on mind-body connection, Pilates promotes awareness and control, offering a supportive environment for building strength and improving overall fitness.

Functional Movement

Functional movement training focuses on exercises that mimic everyday activities, enhancing strength, stability, and mobility in a practical way. This approach emphasizes movements like squatting, lifting, pushing, and pulling, which improve overall body mechanics and coordination. By engaging multiple muscle groups and promoting balance, functional movement helps prevent injury and enhances athletic performance.

Boxing

Boxing classes offer an energetic and engaging way to improve fitness, build strength, and enhance coordination. Participants learn fundamental techniques, including footwork, punches, and defensive strategies, while also benefiting from high-intensity workouts that boost endurance and agility.

Stretching and Foam Roller

Stretching and foam roller classes focus on improving flexibility, mobility, and muscle recovery. Participants engage in guided stretching routines that target major muscle groups, promoting relaxation and reducing tension. The use of foam rollers helps release tightness and knots in the muscles, enhancing circulation and aiding recovery.
